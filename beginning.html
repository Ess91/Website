How and Why?

I graduated from my first degree about 8 years ago in International Business & Globalisation. Pretty much applied to every job role I wanted, majority of the time I got rejection emails after rejection emails, main reason due to lack of experience.

I got a job in retail as a customer assistant hoping that I would only be there for at least 6 months, nope...been there 8 years now..wow! you might be thinking. During that time, I manage to do my Masters in Property Finance, did pretty well. Long story short, I managed to find work experience as an assistant surveyor....pretty good, right? Not really lol...my mentor/friend reckoned that my skills were best suited for the technology field, that maybe I should look into web development....I wasn't so sure but I did look into this.

It has been almost a year since I started this journey, I've really researched on the different bootcamps that was available in the UK, (yes, I'm from London which I forgot to mention), whether it be online or on campus? How will it work around my child? The cost? All the free online resources? Yes, there was a lot to think about but had no clue as to where to begin! I eventually did choose an online bootcamp but made sure I continued using the free resources that was available.

I wanted to start this blog pretty much to document my journey. Although, I'm enjoying the new things in web development, self-doubt seems to be creeping in which has definitely heightened my anxiety. This blog will definitely show me that "Yes Esther, I am doing excellent for a beginner and there's nothing to worry about"!

I'm also hoping this blog helps someone out there that may be lost or may not know where to start. I'll definitely be writing about my experiences so far, all the free online resources and many more (which I can't think of right now lol). If I can do it, you definitely can do it.
If you have any questions (although I'm not an expert yet lol!) or some suggestions, do drop me an email (located in the 'About' page) and I will definitely get back to you!!
