Celebrating the Small Wins

9th November 2020
It’s been a while since I last posted a blog post and launched my website (pretty cool, right?). A lot has happened since launching which for me is pretty strange but exciting at the same time. When launching my website, it was a pretty big deal for me. I was asked how I felt. The honest answer, pretty scary because I’m now out there for everyone to see, I’ve officially left my comfort zone and out in the open but it’s exciting because I’m starting something new.

I was reading a Bible plan by Joyce Meyer called Do It Afraid (my new motto) and it definitely pushed me!


DO IT AFRAID!!

Since launching my website and a little networking too, I was asked very last minute to speak at a webinar briefly on the topic of why ladies should get into coding? Me?!?! I didn’t want to do it! Anyone that knows me, knows I don’t do public speaking at all! I get shy and nervous and all sweaty lol! But I remembered my motto: Do It Afraid! I did it in the end (it did take a lot of convincing though), you could tell I was nervous because I was talking so fast and ended up not knowing what to say, so I thought!

The very next day, one of the participants from the webinar asked if I could be their mentor. Me?!?! Again, I thought I’m not better suited, I didn’t think I knew much, I did ask someone close to me their opinion and the one thing that kept coming up was Do It Afraid! If I could teach someone a thing or two, why not?

I’ve been trying to keep myself busy, blogging, mentoring and creating fun projects too. I’m also taking part in the #100DaysOfCode which you should check out my twitter.

I’m amazed at how far I’ve come, I’m super proud of myself and I’m celebrating the small wins!
